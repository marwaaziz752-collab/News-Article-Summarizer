Troubleshooting Tips
For URL-based summarization:
  1. Verify the URL is correct and accessible
  2. Ensure the website is not blocking content extraction
  3. Try a different news article
  4. Check your internet connection
For text-based summarization:
  1. Ensure your text is at least 20 words long
  2. Check that the text is properly formatted
  3. Avoid pasting very short excerpts
